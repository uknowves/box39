The Founder
VLAD BOX39

Vlad started in a garage in Saint Petersburg. Over 14 years he turned a personal obsession with aesthetics into a brand the whole world copies.

He looks at a motorcycle the way someone does when what matters is how an object exists in space — how it reads standing still, how it reads in motion.

That perspective turned out to be exactly what the industry was missing. Which is why his bikes are bought by people drawn not just to the machine, but to the aesthetics of BOX39.
